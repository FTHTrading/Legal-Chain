## IMPORTANT — READ BEFORE USE

UNYKORN // LAW is a legal intelligence platform. By using this system, you acknowledge the following risks and limitations.

---

## I. AI LIMITATIONS

1. **AI-generated content may contain errors.** All AI outputs are drafts that require human review before use.
2. **AI may produce incorrect legal citations.** Citation validation is automated but not infallible. All citations must be independently verified before reliance.
3. **AI confidence scores are estimates.** A high confidence score does not guarantee correctness.
4. **AI cannot replace legal judgment.** The system assists attorneys — it does not practice law.

## II. DATA RISKS

1. **Client-side storage is not permanent.** The current implementation uses browser localStorage. Data may be lost on browser cache clear.
2. **No warranty of data availability.** System uptime is not guaranteed.
3. **Evidence integrity depends on proper ingestion.** Hash verification only confirms the file hasn't changed since upload — it does not verify the original source's authenticity.

## III. BLOCKCHAIN RISKS

1. **On-chain settlement is final.** ATP transactions on Apostle Chain cannot be reversed after confirmation.
2. **Blockchain forensic tracing provides evidence, not proof.** Wallet ownership attribution requires additional verification.
3. **Cross-chain bridge analysis may miss intermediate hops.** Complex laundering paths may require extended investigation.
4. **Chain data accuracy depends on indexer reliability.** Block explorers and indexers can have gaps or delays.

## IV. LEGAL PRACTICE RISKS

1. **This platform is not a law firm.** Use of this system does not create an attorney-client relationship.
2. **Deadlines are tracked, not guaranteed.** Calendar entries and deadline tracking are aids — not substitutes for independent deadline management.
3. **Jurisdiction analysis requires human verification.** Automated jurisdiction research may miss local rules, standing orders, or recent amendments.
4. **Conflict checks are based on available data.** The system can only check conflicts against parties and entities already in the system.

## V. AGENT NETWORK RISKS

1. **Agents may produce low-quality output.** This is why all agent output goes through human approval gates.
2. **Agent escalation depends on proper threshold calibration.** Escalation triggers at confidence < 0.60 may not catch all edge cases.
3. **Agent actions are logged but not reversible.** Once an action is audit-logged, the log entry is permanent.

## VI. OPERATIONAL RISKS

1. **System depends on third-party services.** LLM providers, blockchain nodes, and hosting infrastructure are external dependencies.
2. **Security vulnerabilities may exist.** Despite threat modeling and mitigation, no system is immune to all attacks.
3. **Regulatory landscape changes.** Legal technology regulations vary by jurisdiction and may change.
