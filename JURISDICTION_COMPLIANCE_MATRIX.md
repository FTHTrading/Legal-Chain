| Control | Requirement |
|---|---|

| Source of Wealth | Sovereign wealth fund mandate, constitutional authority, legislative basis |
| Sanctions Screening | OFAC SDN, UN Consolidated List, EU Consolidated List, OFSI (UK), UAE CBUAE list |
| Beneficial Ownership | Ultimate beneficial ownership to natural person level — in line with FATF R.10 CDD |
| STR Assessment | MAS/FIU/FinCEN reporting assessment at intake and at each significant development |
| Ongoing Monitoring | Semi-annual refresh of KYC/AML profile |
