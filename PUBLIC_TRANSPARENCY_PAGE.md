## What UNYKORN // LAW Is

UNYKORN // LAW is a legal intelligence operating system that accelerates legal operations through AI-assisted workflows. It provides:

- **Case management** — Matter tracking, evidence vaults, claim analysis
- **Document workflows** — Drafting, review, and approval pipelines
- **Legal research** — Multi-jurisdiction authority discovery with citation validation
- **Blockchain forensics** — Wallet tracing, transaction analysis, evidence packaging
- **Client portals** — Secure namespace-based case visibility for clients and families
- **Operations console** — Centralized dashboard for legal team operations

## What UNYKORN // LAW Is NOT

- **Not a law firm** — This is infrastructure, not legal representation
- **Not autonomous** — No AI system sends, files, or publishes without human approval
- **Not a replacement for attorneys** — All legal decisions require human judgment
- **Not a guarantor of outcomes** — Legal analysis includes confidence scores, not promises

## How AI Is Used

| Activity | AI Role | Human Role |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Document drafting | Generates initial drafts | Reviews, edits, approves |
| Legal research | Discovers authorities | Validates citations, applies judgment |
| Forensic analysis | Traces wallet flows | Interprets findings, packages evidence |
| Case intake | Scores urgency, checks conflicts | Reviews classification, accepts cases |
| Communications | Drafts messages | Reviews, approves, sends |

**Critical rule**: No AI-generated content leaves the system without explicit human approval.

## Data Handling

- All case data is stored in isolated matter-level containers
- Evidence items are SHA-256 hashed on ingestion
- Audit logs are hash-chained for tamper detection
- Client portal access is individually permissioned
- No data is shared between unrelated matters

## Confidence Scoring

All AI-generated analysis includes confidence ratings:
- **High (0.85–1.0)**: Strong authority, verified facts
- **Moderate (0.60–0.84)**: Reasonable basis, some gaps
- **Low (0.35–0.59)**: Speculative, limited authority
- **Insufficient (0.0–0.34)**: Cannot be relied upon

Content below 0.60 confidence is automatically flagged for human review.

## Agent Network

The system operates 26 AI agents organized into specialized teams (control, execution, intelligence, interface, document, forensic, communication, research, intake). All agent actions are:
- Logged immutably in the audit trail
- Inspectable by authorized users
- Subject to human approval gates
- Bounded by the system constitution
